Wireframe Edge Joinery & Connectors

Generates connectors and end-caps for wireframe structures, treating mesh edges as physical boards or rods. Choose from six joinery styles — miter, spider, ball, balloon, fuller, or octopus — and export connector geometry for cutting or printing.

How It Works

Turn any mesh into a buildable wireframe structure

Miter

Classic 45° mitered cuts where boards meet at each vertex — clean and simple for rectilinear structures.

Spider & Ball

Radial spoke or spherical hub connectors at each vertex, with cylindrical members along each edge.

Balloon

Animated assembly: edges bend and snap together in sequence. Use the time slider to preview the assembly motion.

Fuller & Octopus

Flat interlocking boards with cylindrical connectors, or hub-and-spoke connector patterns for more complex vertex geometry.

Board Dimensions

Set board width and height to match your stock. The connector geometry is generated to fit your exact material cross-section.

Export

Print Parts exports all connector and member geometry as flat cut files, ready for laser or CNC.
