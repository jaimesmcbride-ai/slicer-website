How It Works

Automatic plate detection and joinery generation

Auto Detection

The plate detection algorithm runs automatically on load, identifying flat faces and grouping them into plates.

Joinery Types

Choose from finger joints, dovetails, or dowels. Detection tolerances are tunable if the algorithm needs help with your model.

Disassembly Animation

Drag the time slider to animate plates separating in joinery order — verify the assembly sequence makes physical sense before cutting.

Part Numbers

Toggle part number labels on each plate to match the cut layout to the 3D view during assembly.

Debug Pipeline

Step through the detection pipeline stage by stage to inspect and tune the intermediate results.

Export

Outputs flat cut-file layouts as PDF or EPS, ready for laser cutting or CNC routing.
